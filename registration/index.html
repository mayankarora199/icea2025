Registration Information

Registration Policy

  • Each Accepted Paper at ICEA 2024 must have at least one author registered with the Author rate at the camera ready submission deadline of October 7, 2024 November 15, 2024.

  • Each Accepted Regular registration can cover 1 Regular Paper.


1. PAYMENT INFORMATION

Registration and payments are handled by the Confit system. Two methods of payment are supported:

  • Credit Card (international)

  • Bank Transfer (Japan only)

Registration link:

  • https://icea.confit.atlas.jp/
