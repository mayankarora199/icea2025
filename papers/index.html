Call for Papers

Introduction

ACM ICEA 2025 is dedicated to fostering the latest innovations and advancements in both Electrical Engineering and Computer Science. The conference serves as a premier platform for researchers, engineers, and industry experts to share groundbreaking ideas, novel methodologies, and transformative technologies shaping the future of these disciplines.

Within Electrical Engineering, the conference will focus on topics such as power systems, renewable energy integration, smart grid technologies, embedded systems, and Internet of Things (IoT) applications. It aims to address the latest advancements in energy-efficient designs, wireless communication, and next-generation electronic devices.

In the realm of Computer Science, ACM ICEA 2025 will highlight key developments in artificial intelligence, machine learning, cybersecurity, quantum computing, and high-performance computing. The event will also provide discussions on cutting-edge areas such as edge computing, cloud computing, big data analytics, blockchain applications, and emerging trends in automation.

The conference encourages interdisciplinary research that bridges Electrical Engineering and Computer Science to solve real-world challenges. Participants will have the opportunity to engage in dynamic discussions, present their findings, and collaborate with leading experts across academia and industry.

Submission

Authors are invited to submit 4-6 page technical papers presenting original, unpublished research, not currently under review elsewhere. 2-page work-in-progress (WIP) works are also accepted. If you wish to submit a WIP work, please include '[WIP]' at the beginning of your paper title.

All paper submissions will be electronic, in PDF format. For more details, please visit the submission page.

Paper Publication

All papers will be fully refereed and undergo a single-blind review process by at least three referees. Accepted papers will be published in the International Conference Proceedings Series by ACM.
